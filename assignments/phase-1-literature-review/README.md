# Phase 1 — Literature Review

## Overview

The Phase 1 assignment is a systematic literature review on the topic of **human-computer collaborative learning (HCCL) in Chinese higher education**.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Word count** | 5,000 – 8,000 words (excluding references) |
| **Format** | `.md` (draft) → `.pdf` (final submission) |
| **Due date** | — (confirm with instructor) |
| **Submission** | Tag final version as `phase1-final` in git |

---

## Objectives

1. Map the existing body of literature on HCCL, identifying key themes, theories, and methodologies.
2. Identify research gaps that will motivate the Phase 2 research proposal.
3. Develop shared theoretical vocabulary for the group's research.
4. Practice systematic literature review methodology (e.g., PRISMA framework).

---

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Search strategy & coverage | 20% | Systematic, well-documented, multi-database search |
| Critical analysis | 30% | Not just summary — compare, contrast, synthesize |
| Thematic organization | 20% | Clear thematic structure with logical flow |
| Identification of gaps | 20% | Clearly articulated research gaps |
