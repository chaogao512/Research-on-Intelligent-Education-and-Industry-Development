# Phase 2 — Research Proposal

## Overview

The Phase 2 assignment is a full research proposal for an original empirical or design study on HCCL in Chinese higher education, building directly on the Phase 1 literature review.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Word count** | 8,000 – 12,000 words (excluding references) |
| **Format** | `.md` (draft) → `.pdf` (final submission) |
| **Due date** | — (confirm with instructor) |
| **Submission** | Tag final version as `phase2-final` in git |

---

## Objectives

1. Develop a clear, focused, and feasible research question grounded in the Phase 1 literature review.
2. Design a rigorous methodology appropriate for the research question.
3. Demonstrate understanding of ethical considerations in educational research.
4. Produce a document suitable for submission as a conference paper or funding proposal draft.

---

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Research question clarity | 20% | Well-defined, focused, and answerable |
| Literature grounding | 20% | Builds clearly on Phase 1 findings |
| Methodology rigor | 25% | Appropriate design, justified choices, addresses validity |
| Ethical considerations | 15% | Thorough treatment of research ethics |
| Feasibility | 10% | Realistic scope, timeline, and resources |
| Formatting & citation | 10% | APA 7th ed., clear structure |
