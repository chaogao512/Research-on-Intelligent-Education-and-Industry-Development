1. Analyze the current landscape of the EdTech industry in China and globally.

3. Identify market trends, investment patterns, and policy drivers shaping EdTech development.

5. Critically assess career pathways and opportunities in the intelligent education sector.

## 🔑 Key Concepts

- **EdTech (Educational Technology Industry)**: Commercial sector developing software, hardware, and services for education.
- **Digital Campus (数字校园)**: University-wide digital transformation initiatives integrating AI, IoT, and cloud services.
- **Intelligent Classroom (智慧课堂)**: Technology-enhanced classrooms with real-time analytics and interactive tools.
- **MOOC Platforms**: Massively Open Online Courses — key Chinese platforms include Xuetangx, NetEase Cloud Classroom, Zhihuishu.
- **"Double Reduction" Policy (双减政策)**: Chinese government policy (2021) restricting K-12 tutoring industry, reshaping EdTech investment.
- **HE Digital Transformation**: The "十四五" (14th Five-Year Plan) targets for higher education informatization.
