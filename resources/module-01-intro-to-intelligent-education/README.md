# Module 01 — Introduction to Intelligent Education

> **《智能教育导论》**

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

1. Define intelligent education and distinguish it from traditional e-learning and computer-based training.
2. Trace the historical development of educational technology from CAI to modern AI-driven systems.
3. Identify the core components of an intelligent education ecosystem (learner model, domain model, pedagogical model, interface).
4. Critically evaluate global trends in intelligent education policy and practice.
5. Connect theoretical frameworks to concrete implementation challenges in Chinese higher education.

## 🔑 Key Concepts

- **Intelligent Tutoring Systems (ITS)**: Automated systems that provide personalized instruction.
- **Adaptive Learning**: Dynamically adjusting content difficulty based on learner performance.
- **Learning Analytics**: Measuring and analyzing learner data to optimize learning outcomes.
- **Educational Data Mining (EDM)**: Applying data mining techniques to educational datasets.
- **AIED (AI in Education)**: The intersection of artificial intelligence research and educational practice.

## 📖 Recommended Readings

1. Bloom, B. S. (1984). The 2 sigma problem: The search for methods of group instruction as effective as one-to-one tutoring. *Educational Researcher*, 13(6), 4–16.
2. Baker, R. S., & Siemens, G. (2014). Educational data mining and learning analytics. In Sawyer, R. K. (Ed.), *Cambridge Handbook of the Learning Sciences* (2nd ed.).
3. Roll, I., & Wylie, R. (2016). Evolution and revolution in artificial intelligence in education. *International Journal of Artificial Intelligence in Education*, 26(2), 582–599.
